How Does This Work?

When a person is willing to donate his blood, he needs to register in this site along with his address, area phone numbers. During crisis in and around your area, the person who need blood will be able to contact you after having searched the site. This helps the time in acquiring blood, and the speed at which the blood is available helps save the patients life.
